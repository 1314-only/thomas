I don’t know if you’ll ever come here and read this one day… Maybe after a long, exhausting day, when life feels a little too heavy? When you’re looking back on the memories we made together? Maybe after all the reflections? Or maybe started to forget me and my voice… I hate admitting this, but I still keep this website alive. It’s really embarrassing, knowing you might never come back to it and I don’t want to keep my voice on here anymore. But somehow, it is the only place where I still feel connect to you.

If that day ever comes, I hope you’ll know that these are the words I wanted to leave behind that I never had the chance to say - the bottom of my heart.

I was completely messed up.

For those eight days without hearing from you, I kept trying to convince myself that everything would be alright. I told myself that, at the very least, I’d try to see you as a part of movitation to keep going through everything that I was facing. While you were quiet, I never stopped thinking about you and everything you might have been going through. I kept wondering whether you were alright (even spreaded some tarot cards searching for something to hold on to and saw you have to carry a lots of things on your shoulder, that you needed to stay quiet, observe, and spend time reflecting and working through things within yoursel), and I telling myself that you’d make it through somehow, because you are Thomas. And congrats, you did it. You are stronger than me, both when I was 17 and now.

But that uncertainty is part of what I have to accept even I don’t want to. The outcome wasn’t the one I had imagined, and for a while, I didn’t know what to do, so I ends up writing everything I want to tell you as we are always direct to each others (I’m so clingy, sorry). I tried to act cool and act like I’m fine when I replied you with only 7 words (not enough space to yap). At this point, I am lowkey thinking that you might feel the same as we always do…

Now, I don’t really know what you’re planning for your future. Maybe you’ll go back to K-pop. Maybe you’ll go to college, like you once talked about. Or maybe, before any of that, you just need time to move on from everything that’s happened. Actually, there’s something I’ve never told you. The selfish part of me had this thought while I was watching Hải rehearse for Live Stage 2. When he performed his dance solo and completely lit up the stage, I caught a glimpse of you in him because you showed me the video where you did that choreography, and I still remember it clearly. For a moment, I couldn’t hold back my tears, because all I could think was, “One day, that could’ve been you”. I had always imagined seeing you shine like that one day… with your youth, your talent and your hard work you’ve put in over 5 years. And by then, I could proudly look at you from backstage, or frontstage as a fan.

Still then, the planets are moving. The season will change. I hope that wherever life takes you, you’ll find the best you were searching for. And I hope I’ll find mine too. I guess it will take me quite a long time to trust someone again but I’ll never regret meeting one of the most special people I’ve ever known.
